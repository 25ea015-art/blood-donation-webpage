Donor Eligibility & Process FAQ

Who can donate?

  • Generally healthy individuals.
  • Age 18 to 65 years.
  • Weight above 50 kg.
  • Must not have donated in the last 90-112 days.

What is the process?

  • Registration: Quick sign-up.
  • Health Check: Mini-physical exam.
  • Donation: Takes about 8-10 minutes.
  • Recovery: 10-15 minutes rest with refreshments.
